About Us

Green Mayors offers innovative solutions to clients from diverse industries globally, based on over two decades of experience. Our offering has an impressive portfolio of professional and technical services, with a focus on enriching your business, with minimal commercial and operational overheads.

We aim to offer a wide range of IT Solutions, Services and Products that are highly innovative yet cost-effective. We also aim at making technology more accessible and affordable to every business sector, and especially to the non-tech savvy business owners. Few of the industries we focus on and have a stack of solutions and services are Telco, Healthcare, Education, Oil & Gas, Energy, Government sector, Retail, Logistics and Retails industries.

Over a period of time, we have developed expertise through collaboration and partnership in providing solutions and services to businesses that are easy to plug into an existing platform and less overhead. We understand that not all businesses are the same, and with this in mind, we strive to offer an exceptional solution tailored to your needs.
